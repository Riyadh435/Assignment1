Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Democracy

Ensures people's sovereignty through voting and transparent governance.

Nationalism

Reflects pride in the nation's history, culture, and independence.

Socialism

Advocates for social justice and equitable wealth distribution.

Secularism

Promotes religious harmony and equality among all citizens.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Bangladesh

Advancing technology and innovation to create a knowledge-based economy.

Economic Prosperity

Transforming into a middle-income nation with industrial and agricultural growth.

Global Harmony

Fostering peace, cooperation, and equality among nations for a better world.
